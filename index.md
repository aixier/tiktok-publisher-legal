# OpenClaw TikTok Publisher

Legal documents for **OpenClaw TikTok Publisher**, a desktop productivity tool that helps content creators upload video drafts from their local workspace to their own TikTok drafts box via TikTok's official Content Posting API.

## Legal Documents

- [**Terms of Service**](./terms) — Your rights and responsibilities when using the application
- [**Privacy Policy**](./privacy) — How we handle your data

## About the Application

OpenClaw TikTok Publisher is an open-source desktop extension for the [OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw) platform. It integrates with TikTok's official developer APIs to help individual creators:

- Authorize their own TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
- Upload video drafts from their local workspace to their TikTok drafts inbox
- Review and publish those drafts from within the TikTok mobile application

**Key principles**:

- ✅ Uses only **official TikTok Content Posting API**
- ✅ **User-authorized**: OAuth 2.0 with the creator's own account
- ✅ **User-controlled publishing**: Final publish happens inside the TikTok mobile app
- ✅ **Local-first**: Access tokens stored on the user's own device (permissions 0600)
- ✅ **No third-party data sharing**
- ✅ **AI-generated content** is always tagged `is_aigc=true` per TikTok policy
- ❌ No automation, no scheduling, no multi-account management
- ❌ No scraping, no reverse-engineered APIs
